About Me

Hi, I'm Kiki!

I love drawing characters in chibi and gacha life style — big heads, bigger expressions, and outfits I'd probably never wear in real life but wish I could.

Just as much as drawing, I love making stories. I like giving every character a reason for being on the page — a personality, a rivalry, a soft spot — before I ever draw their outfit.
